Alright, so here's the thing about building tools for yourself. You spend all this time getting the transcription working, and then you realize the bottleneck isn't even the model anymore, it's all the stuff around it. Like the time it takes from pressing the hotkey to actually starting the recording, and then from stopping to getting the text pasted. That's the bit that matters. Because when you're in flow and you just want to capture a thought, every second of latency pulls you out of it. So yeah, the goal here is to make it feel instant. Press the button, talk, press it again, and the text is just there. No waiting, no spinning, just done. And the quality has to be good enough that you don't need to go back and fix it every time. That's the whole point.
